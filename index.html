Profile

Where scholarship
meets practice

I am a Senior Lecturer, researcher, and active industry practitioner with over a decade of experience across higher education and technology. I hold a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Leicester and an MSc in Management Information Systems from the University of Hertfordshire.

My academic career spans curriculum design, module leadership, and quality assurance at institutions including Elizabeth School of London. I design and deliver structured, outcome-driven programmes that bridge academic rigour with employability — evidenced by measurable improvements in student progression and graduate outcomes.

Alongside my academic role, I maintain an active practice as a Senior Android Engineer, currently building scalable mobile systems using Kotlin and Jetpack Compose. This dual engagement ensures that my teaching reflects the realities of contemporary software development, bringing live industry standards into the classroom.

My research specialises in AI, deep learning, and edge deployment — areas in which I also consult, helping organisations move machine learning from research prototype to production-ready systems.
